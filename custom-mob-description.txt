BABY ENDERMAN CUSTOM MINECRAFT MOB

HOW DOES IT SPAWN?
- Spawn egg
- The egg is small, and black, with purple particles on the surface

HOW BIG IS IT?
- Outer max dimensions: 6x6x8 px (width, depth, height) -- half-size baby

WHAT SHAPE IS IT? 
- It is a baby Enderman.

HOW DOES IT MOVE?
- It walks like a normal Enderman. It is not able to teleport.

WHAT DOES IT DO?
- It is a companion. It can be tamed.
- Until it is tame, it will just walk around and do occasional cute little jumps.
- The baby Enderman is able to carry blocks just like a normal Enderman. It can carry grass, stone, dirt and sand blocks.
- It is able to pick up any of the above blocks from its environment at random.
- Unlike normal Enderman, baby Enderman cannot harm the player

HOW DO I INTERACT WITH IT?
- Once tame and sitting on your shoulder, the baby Enderman will occasionally gift the player random blocks from the above list
- The baby Enderman can be tamed by giving it three emeralds (green gems for its green eyes; not ender pearls -- throwing a pearl teleports the player!)
- Once it is tame, it will sit on your player's shoulder.

WHAT VERSION OF MINECRAFT?
- Java Edition
- 1.21.11
- Neoforge 21.11.42
